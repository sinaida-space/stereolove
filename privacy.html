Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

1. Who operates this site

Stereolove is an experimental web installation by Sinaida Krivchenko, a Prague-based visual artist and digital strategist working globally. Contact information and professional links are available at https://sinaida.eu/.

2. What this site collects

The Stereolove application does not include analytics, advertising pixels, mailing list forms, account systems, or tracking cookies.

If you enable camera mode, the browser asks for camera permission. Camera frames are used locally in your browser to estimate face position for the visual effect. The application does not intentionally record, store, or upload camera images.

The optional sound layer is generated locally with the browser Web Audio API after you choose an interaction mode. The site does not request microphone access and does not record audio.

3. Essential storage

The site uses local storage only to remember that you closed the cookie notice. This is necessary for a stable user experience and is not used to identify you across other sites.

4. Third-party services

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may process basic technical data such as IP address, browser information, request time, and requested files in server logs.

Camera mode loads MediaPipe browser assets from public CDNs and Google-hosted model files. These requests may expose standard technical request data to those providers. Camera mode starts only after you press the camera button and grant browser permission.

5. International providers

GitHub, CDN providers, and Google-hosted MediaPipe assets may operate infrastructure outside the European Economic Area. Where applicable, those providers are responsible for their own transfer mechanisms and safeguards.

6. Legal basis under GDPR

Essential local storage is processed on the basis of legitimate interest in providing a functional website. Camera access is based on your explicit browser permission and can be withdrawn at any time by stopping camera mode or changing browser permissions.

Generated audio cues are played only after your interaction with the artwork and do not involve personal data collection.

7. Your rights

If GDPR applies to you, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or portability for personal data, where applicable. Because this site does not run accounts or analytics, most personal data related to visits is limited to technical hosting logs controlled by hosting providers.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. Retention

The application itself does not retain camera data or visitor profiles. Browser local storage remains on your device until you clear site data. Hosting providers may retain technical logs according to their own operational policies.

9. No sale of personal data

This site does not sell personal data and does not use visitor data for advertising profiling.

10. Contact

For privacy questions about this artwork, use the contact links published on sinaida.eu.
