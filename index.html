Stereolove

Stereolove is a perceptual instrument: it turns a flat monitor into an unstable optical volume and asks how much of reality is made by the position of the observer.

Move slowly. The questions appear as constellations of light, not as fixed answers. The work is about noticing how attention, intimacy, memory, and viewpoint edit the world.
